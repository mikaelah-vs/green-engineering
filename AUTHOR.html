About the Author

Mikaelah Villalon Sebastian is a first-year Civil Engineering student at Western Mindanao State University. She is passionate about promoting green and sustainable construction of infrastructures. With a growing awareness of the environmental impacts of 'grey infrastructure', particularly its contribution to pollution, she advocates for eco-friendly materials, innovative technologies, and ethical engineering practices.

She believes that engineers play a vital role in shaping a sustainable future and aims to contribute to infrastructure development that focuses on strength, safety, cost-efficiency, and environmental friendly and responsibility for present and future generations.
