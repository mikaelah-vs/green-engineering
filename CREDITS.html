Green Infrastructure

Engineering Standards & Manuals

ASCE/COS 73-23: Standard Practice for Sustainable Infrastructure. The primary standard from the American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE) defining performance-based outcomes for sustainable infrastructure throughout its lifecycle.

ASCE/EWRI 78-24: Guidelines for Water, Wastewater, and Stormwater Utilities. Updated in 2024, this provides specific engineering guidance for managing modern utility systems, including green-grey hybrids.

Program:Envision Framework (Institute for Sustainable Infrastructure). A holistic rating system (similar to LEED but for infrastructure) used by engineers to evaluate the "co-benefits" and resilience of green projects.

Regulatory & Design Guidelines

US EPA Green Infrastructure Modeling Toolkit (2025/2026 Update). A suite of tools including the Storm Water Management Model (SWMM) and the National Stormwater Calculator (SWC), essential for modeling infiltration and peak flow attenuation.

EPA Bioretention Design Handbook: A practical guide for engineers on designing holistic bioretention cells, focusing on performance, soil media specifications, and long-term longevity.

NOAA Office for Coastal Management: Green Infrastructure Options to Reduce Flooding. Technical resources and "Quick Reference" guides for implementing nature-based solutions to mitigate flood risk in urban and coastal environments.

Research & Textbooks

Lens, P. N. L., & Bui, X. T. (Eds.). (2025). Nature-Based Solutions for Urban Sustainability. IWA Publishing. A comprehensive textbook covering the process fundamentals of hydrodynamics, media interactions, and phytoremediation in green systems.

Rusnak, C. R., et al. (2025). "Sustainable Construction Materials and Green Infrastructure." Mesopotamian Journal of Civil Engineering. A bibliometric and technical analysis of environmental innovation in civil engineering, including low-carbon binders and permeable materials.

"Cost of Maintaining Green Infrastructure" (ASCE Library). A vital resource for long-term asset management that quantifies maintenance expenses and operational requirements for "living assets."

Academic Journals for Current Trends

Journal of Sustainable Water in the Built Environment (ASCE). Focuses specifically on the engineering and environmental aspects of urban stormwater.

Landscape and Urban Planning. Peer-reviewed research on the integration of biodiversity and ecosystem services into urban infrastructure design.
